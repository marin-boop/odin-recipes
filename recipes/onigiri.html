Onigiri


Description

Onigiri – These adorable Japanese rice balls are simple, comforting, and absolutely delicious. Filled with tasty ingredients and wrapped in nori, they’re the perfect snack or lunch on the go.

ingredients

  • japanese short-grain rice
  • nori sheets

Steps

  1. wash the rice gently
  2. cook the rice until its done
  3. transfer the freshly cook rice into a large bowl and season it with salt and pepper
  4. shape your rice into a round ball or a triangle
  5. add on a sheet of nori
