Bratkartoffeln (Fried Potatoes)


Description

Bratkartoffeln are a classic German side dish made from sliced or diced potatoes, pan-fried until golden and crispy. Often cooked with onions and sometimes bacon, theyâ€™re simple yet flavorful. Served hot, they pair well with eggs, sausages, or as a hearty addition to any meal.

ingredients

  • waxy boiled potatoes
  • onion
  • eggs
  • butter
  • oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. cut potatoes into round slices
  2. fry onions on medium heat till they soften
  3. add the sliced potatoes
  4. mix well und fry until colden and crispy
  5. pour mixed eggs over the potatoes
  6. season with salt and pepper
  7. cook until the eggs are done but not too dry
  8. sprinkle some herbs
  9. enjoy!
