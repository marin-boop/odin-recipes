Šaltibarščiai (Cold Beet Soup)


Description

Šaltibarščiai is a traditional Lithuanian cold soup made with vibrant pink beetroot, kefir, cucumbers, dill, and green onions. It's refreshingly tangy, creamy, and typically served chilled with a side of boiled potatoes. Perfect for hot summer days, this soup is both healthy and packed with flavor.

ingredients

  • boiled potatoes
  • beetroot
  • cucumbers
  • spring onions
  • hard boiled eggs
  • kefir
  • fresh dill

Steps

  1. chop potatoes into bite-sized pieces
  2. cut beetroot into lengths
  3. chop spring onions
  4. cut dill
  5. transfer everything but the potatoes into a large bowl
  6. pour over the kefir and give it a good mix
  7. season with salt
  8. sprinkle with remaining dill
  9. serve with potatoes and enjoy!
